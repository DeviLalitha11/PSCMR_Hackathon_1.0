About the Hackathon

Open for all Engineering, Diploma, Arts, Science, Architecture, Medicine, Pharmacy, Management students etc.

The hackathon will bring together innovative minds from various disciplines to solve real-world problems in a competitive and collaborative environment. Participants will have 24 hours to develop a working prototype on a chosen theme.
